About Us

Class 12th Papers is a dedicated app designed to help students easily view and download previous year CBSE Class 12 question papers. Whether you're preparing for board exams or brushing up on a subject, this app gives you quick access to authentic and organized question papers.

Our goal is to simplify your preparation process by offering a clean and user-friendly interface, regularly updated with the latest content. Steams covered include Science, Commerce, Arts, Common Subjects, and Other Streams too.

We are passionate about education and believe in empowering every student with the right resources. If you have suggestions, feedback, or questions, we’d love to hear from you!
